Achievements Explanations

- Sniper : Kill all the ennemies without losing a bullet
- Double shot : Kill two ennemies with one bullet
- Triple shot : Kill three ennemies with one bullet
- UFO : Kill the special alien
- Self sabotage : Destroy every last rock of each bunker
- Bonus needed : Finish a level by collecting all bonus
- Who needs bonus : Finish a level without collecting any bonus
- Perfect : Finish a level without losing a life
- Bullet Wall : Destroy a bullet of an alien
- Highest ground : Go to the last level
- PGM : Go to the last level without losing one life
